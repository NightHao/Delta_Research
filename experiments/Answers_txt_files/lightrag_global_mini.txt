   - The SECC manages the communication between the EV and the EVSE, ensuring that the protocols defined in standards like ISO 15118 are followed during the charging process.

4. **Electric Vehicle Communication Controller (EVCC)**:
   - The EVCC is responsible for managing the communication protocols within the EV, facilitating the interaction with the SECC and ensuring compliance with V2G standards.
